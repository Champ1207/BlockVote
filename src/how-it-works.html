How does BlockVote Works ?

by CS-A TY Group 18

Standing in the queue and waiting for your turn to cast our vote is a part from history now.

TY group 18 of VIT CS proposes a new system of voting where everything is transparent and no question of tampering the voting machine's as they doesn't exists in first place.

BlockVote is a upcoming way of casting vote where all the votes will be stored in the Blockchain network, which makes the system tamper proof and fully transparent with a high level of Security.

This is still a project in development! Please expect a few drawbacks and up & downs

How to vote here?

1. Install the MetaMask Browser Extension.

2. Make sure that the Ethereum Account which you've been assigned is imported into MetaMask wallet.

3. Visit the Cast Vote Page and Selecting the candidate you can place your Vote.
